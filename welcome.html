Welcome to Meet Brief

The smart way to stay focused and organized during Google Meet calls

Getting Started

Meet Brief helps you capture and summarize your Google Meet conversations. It works quietly in the background to create concise summaries of your meetings, so you can focus on the conversation instead of taking notes.

1

Enable the extension

Click on the Meet Brief icon in your browser's extension toolbar and toggle the switch to enable it.

2

Join a Google Meet call

Meet Brief will automatically detect when you join a Google Meet call.

3

Review your meeting summary

After your meeting ends, Meet Brief will create a summary of key points discussed. You can copy or download the summary for your records.

Key Features

Automatic Summaries

Automatically create concise summaries after each meeting ends.

Meeting History

Keep a record of all your meetings for future reference.

Live Brief View

Access a real-time transcript during your meeting for quick reference.

Customizable Settings

Adjust summary detail levels and other options to suit your needs.

How it works

Meet Brief uses speech recognition technology to capture the conversation in your Google Meet calls. Our intelligent algorithm then identifies key points, action items, and important information to create a concise summary.

The extension runs entirely in your browser, ensuring your meeting data remains private and secure. Your meeting transcripts are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export them.
